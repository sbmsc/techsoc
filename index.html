How to hack hackathons and internships!

This session was given by Deepak Pathania(Developer in Postman) who is a great example of hardwork and success. He told students how they could get into hackathons, what it takes to win them, how to get internships with your skills and many more interesting and rich facts about DevWorld.

31st August 2017

Seminar on Robotics, control systems and Artificial Intelligence!

This event saw young and curious minds in the audience, all of whom had come to watch Mr. Mayank Purushottam speak about the career prospects in Robotics, control systems and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Mr. Pururshottam was previously an Assistant Professor at DTU and is currently a Researcher at the Indian Institute of Agricultural Research.He is currently co-founder and CEO of LBD Robotics. He is a specialist in building drones.

12th March 2018

Career Guidance in Computer Science.

Developing a career in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology can be a tough nut to crack but to make things easier for budding professionals, we had a session on "Growing a career in Computer Science" by Manoj Pandey who will soon join Yelp in Germany and in the past worked and collaborated with some research labs and internships at IIIT-D, Stanford, SquadRun Inc and more. Currently, he organises PyData Delhi meetups and have given a couple of talks in various international Python conferences.
